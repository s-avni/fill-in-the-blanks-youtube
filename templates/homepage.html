Fill in the blanks YouTube generator

This website offers a uniquely easy way to practice a new language. It generates a fill-in the-blank work sheet from a YouTube video of your choosing that you can fill while listening to the video.


Instructions:
  1. Choose the video you want to use for your exercise.
  2. Copy the URL of the video (the website address)
  3. Paste it below and press continue.
  4. On the next page, choose the language you want to practice.
  5. Download the PDF.
  6. While watching the video on YouTube, try to fill in the missing words!
Important!
  • The video you choose must include the option to add subtitles that are not auto-generated. To make sure it does, go to the video, press settings, subtitles, and make sure there is one available.
  • As of now, the website does not support right to left languages :(

Select any YouTube video with (non automatic) captions and instantly generate an exercise & solutions sheet! Have a look at our demo sheet if you want to see an example.
